Explore Lodging in Taniti

Taniti has a wide variety of lodging that ranges from an inexpensive hostel to one large, four-star resort. There are many small, family-owned hotels and a growing number of bed and breakfasts. All types of lodging are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government.

Whether you’re seeking luxury or a budget-friendly option, Taniti offers something for everyone. From beachfront resorts with breathtaking ocean views to cozy cabins nestled in the rainforest, you’ll find the perfect place to relax and recharge. Airbnb options are also available, providing unique stays that let you live like a local.

Types of Lodging

Hotels and Resorts

Taniti features a range of hotels, from small boutique options to the island’s luxurious four-star resort. These accommodations often include amenities such as pools, restaurants, and guided tours. Many are located close to beaches and major attractions.

Airbnb and Vacation Rentals

For a more personalized experience, choose from a variety of Airbnb options. Stay in a charming village home, a modern apartment, or a beachfront villa. Vacation rentals allow you to enjoy privacy while experiencing the island like a local.

Cabins and Eco-Lodges

Adventure seekers can stay in rustic cabins or eco-lodges surrounded by the island’s lush rainforests. These accommodations are ideal for nature lovers and offer a peaceful retreat away from the bustling tourist areas.

Plan Your Stay

No matter where you stay in Taniti, you’re guaranteed warm hospitality, stunning views, and an unforgettable experience. Book in advance to secure your preferred accommodation, especially during peak tourist seasons.

Travel Tips

  • Currency: U.S. dollars
  • Power Outlets: 120 volts (same as U.S.)
  • Language: English is widely spoken among younger locals
  • Climate: Warm and tropical year-round
